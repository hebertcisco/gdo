# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, contributors and maintainers of `gdo` pledge to make participation in the project a respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- be respectful in discussions and review feedback
- assume good intent and focus on the technical work
- accept constructive criticism gracefully
- help keep the project welcoming to new contributors

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- insulting or demeaning language
- deliberate intimidation
- publishing private information without permission
- disruptive conduct that repeatedly derails collaboration

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards and may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, and other contributions that do not align with this code of conduct.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it through a private maintainer channel when one is available. If no dedicated channel has been published yet, open a minimal issue requesting a private contact path without exposing sensitive details publicly.
